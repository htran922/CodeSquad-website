Your future is on the Web

Are you ready for a career change?

Do you like to solve puzzles? Do you wonder how websites and apps are made? Our free, intensive learn-to-code bootcamp can teach you the "full stack" of web development. That includes everything from HTML and CSS to JavaScript, Node, Express, Mongo and more, plus job readiness skills.

Application checklist

To apply to our program, you must:

  • have a high school diploma (or equivalent)
  • be a US citizen or immigrant who is authorized to work in the United States
NO coding experience is required apply, and there is NO maximum age.
Classes are held Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday evenings, 6:30pm-9pm, and Saturdays 9am-2pm, from January through June.

Application Process

Our application process is divided into three phases:

Coding & Career Events

These optional events are a chance for participants to jump right into coding by learning to build a basic web page for a mock "client." The event concludes with a career panel featuring CodeSquad Graduates and software industry volunteers.

For our 2021 recruiting season, all CodeSquad recruiting events will be held online via Zoom.

HTML Workshops

To apply to our 2020 web development bootcamp, all applicants must complete an HTML Workshop. At this Workshop you will learn to create a website with images, multiple pages and a navigation bar, and upload your work to GitHub (a popular website for sharing software code used by professional developers).

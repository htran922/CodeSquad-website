Companies turn to CodeSquad to build their pipeline of entry-level web and mobile developers

CodeSquad is a non-profit organization that trains talented adults from Boston's urban core to become full-stack web developers and launch a new career. Students are trained in web design and server-side development, as well as Agile methodology, effective communication and teamwork. Following their training, CodeSquad works to place graduates in full-time software and technology jobs in greater Boston.

Lena Park Community Development

Founded in 1968, Lena Park provides quality affordable housing, fully-licensed K-5 after-school and youth development programs, and classes in the arts, entrepreneurship, digital fabrication and robotics. Since 2017 Lena Park has partnered with CodeSquad to offer our learn-to-code bootcamp.

Bank of New York Mellon

Alice P. Chase TrustMary D. Whitney Trust

Founded in 1784, the Bank of New York Mellon Corporation provides financial products and services to institutions, corporations, and high net worth individuals in the United States and internationally. BNY Mellon believes that a stronger society means a stronger BNY Mellon. This belief has led them to become a major funder for CodeSquad.
